Matcha iceCream / green tea iceCream

Ingredients:

1. Matcha 15g
2. Yolk 2
3. Sugar 90g
4. Cream 250ml
5. Milk 200ml

Method:

1. Mix sugar, yolk, and milk together.
2. Keep them worm.
3. Put matcha in it.
4. Use machine mix the cream to fine and smooth.
5. Mix cream and matcha mix together.
6. Put into the freezer.
